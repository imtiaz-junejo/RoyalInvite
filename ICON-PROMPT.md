# Eternally Yours — Icon Prompt Guide

Use this file as the reusable prompt for generating new app icons for the wedding invitation platform.

## How to use

1. Copy the **Eternally Yours Base Prompt** below.
2. Send it directly to your image model/designer.
3. Ask for all required export sizes in one request.
4. If needed, ask for one extra monochrome variant for browser tab testing.

---

## Eternally Yours Base Prompt

Create a premium, elegant app icon for Eternally Yours.

### Brand direction
- Product/domain: invite.sikanderkumbhar.com
- Brand name: Eternally Yours
- Brand personality: luxurious, romantic, elegant, timeless, emotionally warm
- Target audience: couples planning weddings who want premium, shareable digital invitations
- Visual style: refined serif-inspired elegance with subtle luxury touches (soft glow, gold accents)

### Design requirements
- Primary motif/symbol: interlocking rings or infinity knot that subtly incorporates a heart and/or envelope/letter shape, representing eternal love and invitation delivery
- Alternative motif: elegant wedding ring with a small sparkle/star detail, or a stylized wax seal with a heart monogram
- Color palette:
  - Primary: `#C9A84C` (luxury gold)
  - Secondary: `#1A1208` (deep espresso/ink)
  - Accent: `#E8D5A3` (champagne/light gold)
- Background: transparent plus one solid dark rounded-square version for app icons
- Corner style: rounded-square
- Style: premium jewelry brand aesthetic — think Cartier/Tiffany minimalism
- Must be clean and readable at very small sizes (16x16, 32x32).
- Avoid tiny details that disappear at favicon size.
- Keep strong contrast for dark and light browser tabs.

### Output constraints
- Deliver a single centered icon mark with generous safe area.
- No text labels in icon.
- Keep composition balanced and symmetric.
- Export files:
  - `favicon.ico` (contains 16, 32, 48)
  - `favicon.png` (512x512)
  - `icon-192.png`
  - `icon-512.png`
  - `apple-touch-icon.png` (180x180)
  - Optional transparent source PNG (1024x1024)

### Variants to generate
- **Variant A**: Interlocking two rings forming an infinity figure with a subtle heart in the negative space (gold on dark background) — best for brand recognition
- **Variant B**: Elegant wax seal shape with a minimalist heart/monogram inside (gold + deep espresso) — premium and wedding-appropriate
- **Variant C**: Simple stylized envelope/letter folded into a heart shape with gold accent lines — communicates "invitation" clearly

Also provide a short rationale for each variant and recommend the best option for favicon readability.

## QA Checklist (before finalizing)

- Looks clear at 16x16 and 32x32.
- No blur/pixelation on browser tab.
- Good contrast on both dark and light backgrounds.
- Visual center is balanced (not drifting left/right).
- Works in square crop without clipping.
- Matches Eternally Yours brand tone: luxurious, romantic, premium.
- Gold elements remain visible and legible at favicon size.
- Reads as "wedding/invitation/luxury" at a glance.
